About competition

EBEC (European BEST Engineering Сompetition) is an engineering competition which gathers students from all across Europe every year. The project's main goal is to provide students with the opportunity to improve their skills and knowledge, display creativity, and learn how to work within a team.

DATE OF THE EVENT

23-30/11/2020

THE CONTEST HAS TWO CATEGORIES

Team Design

The teams must resolve a practical case while having access to a limited amount of resources. The result is expected to be a device, which completes the necessary tasks.

This category's assignment covers general engineering fields: Mechanics, Electronics, Radio engineering, Automation

Case Study

The teams must resolve an analytical case. The result is expected to be a strategy, which provides effective and optimal solutions to the presented problems.

This category's assignment consists of an analysis and examination of a given technical problem.

Career Day

Career Day is organized within the framework of this year's competitions, where Ukraine's leading companies will be participating. Companies have the opportunity to present their internship programs and job opportunities to young university professionals. Communication between students and companies takes place in the form of lectures, training or workshops.
